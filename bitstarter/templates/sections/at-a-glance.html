How it works?

  1. Set the goal
  2. Define the tasks
  3. Collect all ideas about expected result
  4. Share your ideas and let people improve them
  5. Collect resources and tools in one box
  6. Plan your work
  7. Do the tasks
  8. Look at statistics to improve your skills
